Disabling the Automated remote restart option for a VSI prevents the VSI from being restarted automatically on a working host in the event of a host failure. Ensure that your [High availability](#x2284708){: term} (HA) and [Disaster recovery](#x2113280){: term} (DR) policies are configured to protect the workload during a host failure. For more information, see [High availability and disaster recovery](/docs/power-iaas?topic=power-iaas-ha-dr).
